Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping procedures, delivery times, and policies

Shipping Information

At Pro Gear Hub, we strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs, including standard, expedited, and express shipping. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times will be displayed at checkout.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental US.

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international destinations. Please note:

  • International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes which are the responsibility of the customer
  • Delivery times for international orders vary by destination (typically 7-21 business days)
  • Some product restrictions may apply based on destination country regulations

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on the carrier's website.

Important Notes

  • Shipping times are estimates and not guaranteed, as delays may occur due to carrier issues or unforeseen circumstances
  • We are not responsible for lost or stolen packages that are marked as delivered by the carrier
  • Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout, as address changes may delay delivery

Need Help?

If you have any questions about our shipping policy or need assistance with an order, please contact our customer service team at shipping@progearhub.com or call us at (555) 123-4567.
