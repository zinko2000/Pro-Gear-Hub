Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and policies.

Shipping & Delivery

We typically process and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. You'll see the exact shipping cost at checkout after entering your address.

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags attached. Some items like clearance products may be final sale.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number.

Once we receive your returned item, we typically process refunds within 3-5 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Yes, we offer exchanges for size or color if the item is in stock. Please contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

Payment & Pricing

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

We offer price adjustments within 7 days of purchase if the item goes on sale. Please contact our customer service team with your order number and details of the price change.

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your full credit card details on our servers.

Product Questions

Each product page includes a detailed size chart with measurements. For clothing, we recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size charts as sizing may vary by brand.

If you're unsure, our customer service team can help you determine the best size for your needs.

Most of our fitness equipment comes with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product and is listed in the product description. For warranty claims, please contact our customer service team.
